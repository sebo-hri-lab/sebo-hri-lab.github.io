The UChicago HRI lab seeks to share the resources we develop in our research with the broader research community, including questionnaire measures, code, data, and more. Beyond the specific resources listed below, please also check out:

  • The publication page for each of our lab's papers and paper-specific resources,
  • Our lab's GitHub organization for code-related resources, and
  • Our lab's YouTube channel for resaerch and project videos.

Connection-Coordination Rapport (CCR) Scale

A psychometric scale used to measure rapport between two interactants (e.g., a human and a robot).

Interactive IOS Scale for Multiparty Interactions (IIMI)

A measure of interpersonal closeness with multiple interactants adapted from Aron's Inclusion of Other in the Self (IOS) scale.

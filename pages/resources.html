Questionnaire Measures

  • Connection-Coordination Rapport (CCR) Scale

    A psychometric scale used to measure rapport between two interactants (e.g., a human and a robot). We have developed and validated both a full-length 18-item version and a reduced-length 8-item version.

    Scale and Usage →

  • Interactive IOS Scale for Multiparty Interactions (IIMI)

    A measure of interpersonal closeness with multiple interactants adapted from Aron's Inclusion of Other in the Self (IOS) scale.
